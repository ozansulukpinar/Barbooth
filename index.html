Rules

Player starts game.

All bets are 10 points.

Either of the two players wins all

if they throw a 3-3, 5-5 or 6-6.

If they throw 5-6, they win the bet.

They lose if they throw 1-1, 2-2 or 4-4.

If they throw 1-2, they lose the bet.

All other combinations are meaningless.
